08 · Roadmap

52+ week implementation — core phases 1–9 + 4 SOTA phases

Sequenced so each phase produces a standalone deliverable. Exit criteria are the hard gate — no phase promotes to the next until its criteria are green.

  1. W 1–2

    Phase 1 · DNA Foundation

    shipped

    Reproducible extraction pipeline + April 2026 baseline in Neon.

    • DB migrations: dna_video_analyses, dna_profiles
    • Kalodata export + yt-dlp batch download (200 videos)
    • Gemini 2.0 Flash analysis · 42-attribute JSON
    • Synthesis pipeline · correlations + category profiles
    • Weekly cron live (Mon 02:00 PT)
    Exit: baseline in DB · attr coverage ≥ 95% · first 2 cron runs validated
  2. W 3–4

    Phase 2 · Script Generation

    in progress

    ScriptSage aware of hook weights, CTA placement, duration targets.

    • hook_type_weights + cta_placement_pct_target params
    • duration_range_seconds → word count constraint (WPS 2.85)
    • CTA template library · cta_type_weights
    • dna_generation_configs · every job logs config used
    • A/B framework · 10% traffic · DNA vs standard
    Exit: human approval ≥ 80% · A/B split live
  3. W 5–8

    Phase 3 · Video Pipeline Configs

    queued

    dna_vertex, dna_pollo, dna_seedance live; post-processing overrides active.

    • All three DNA configs shipped as additive siblings
    • Post-processing: music_bed=false, burned-in large captions, B-roll ≤ 20%
    • 50 variants per product for dna_vertex; 25 for mid/budget
    • QA 100 generated videos · caption / duration / music checks
    • Canary 10% of new jobs
    Exit: QA pass ≥ 85% · 50 variants clean · canary stable
  4. W 9–10

    Phase 4 · Compliance Scoring

    queued

    7-dim score on 100% of DNA outputs · quality gate at 0.70 composite.

    • Per-dimension scorer · Gemini re-analysis + audio classifier + scene detection
    • Composite weighted average + distribution gate
    • Consume voice-lock composite (≥ 0.88) as second gate
    • Scores persisted to dna_video_analyses
    • Metabase dashboard · score distribution + rejection reasons
    Exit: 100% output coverage · median score ≥ 0.75 · dashboard live
  5. W 11–14

    Phase 5 · Feedback Loop

    queued

    TikTok metrics → bandit updates → weekly improvement.

    • TikTok Business API every 6h → dna_performance_feedback
    • Thompson Sampling bandit · persistence in dna_bandits
    • Cross-category blended priors for low-data categories (< 50 videos)
    • JS-divergence drift detection + Slack alerts
    • Convergence validation over 4-week window
    Exit: 2+ attributes converging · zero false-positive drift alerts
  6. W 15–18

    Phase 6 · Self-Optimization

    queued

    Autonomous loop · zero human touchpoints for a 2-week cycle.

    • Auto-update DNA configs from bandit state (weekly)
    • Baseline auto-reset after 3 consecutive drift weeks
    • Auto-enroll new categories once ≥ 50 videos
    • VideoReward-style scorer vs rule-based compliance (precision test)
    • Flow-DPO preference dataset from bandit outcomes
    Exit: 2-week hands-off run · GMV lift ≥ 15% vs pre-DNA
  7. W 19–22

    Phase 7 · Multi-account & creator-specific DNA

    later

    Per-creator & per-shop profiles on top of category profiles — hierarchical Bayesian blending.

    • dna_profiles_creator table keyed (creator_id, category)
    • Hierarchical prior: category → creator (shrinkage by sample size)
    • Per-shop override UI (brand voice locks, banned claims)
    • Segment-level bandit state · memory-efficient sparse posterior
    • Back-compat: default path stays category-level
    Exit: 20+ creators with ≥ 100 samples each · per-creator mean lift ≥ +5pp
  8. W 23–26

    Phase 8 · Counterfactual & off-policy evaluation

    later

    Retrain offline on historical logs — estimate “what if” rewards before live A/B.

    • Importance-sampled off-policy estimator on dna_generation_configs × dna_performance_feedback
    • Weighted IPS & doubly-robust bounds
    • Nightly “shadow champion” run: proposed policy vs live policy on last 7d logs
    • CI gate: shadow lift must beat live at p < 0.05 before promotion
    • Publish champion-vs-shadow diffs to Slack each morning
    Exit: 4 consecutive weeks with ≥ 1 shadow-promoted policy and zero regressions
  9. W 27–30

    Phase 9 · International & vertical expansion

    later

    Cross-locale profiles and new verticals (Home Appliances, Beauty Tools, Health).

    • locale column on all DNA tables (US, UK, DE, JP pilots)
    • Locale-aware hook taxonomy (problem_pain_uk, social_proof_jp)
    • Language-aware caption_style arms (kana, romaji, umlauts)
    • Compliance rubric locale overrides (music bed rules differ by market)
    • 2 new verticals onboarded with ≥ 50-video baseline each
    Exit: 2 locales live · 2 new verticals live · no US regression
  10. W 31–34

    Phase 10 · SOTA quick wins (Sprint 1)

    SOTA

    5 high-leverage, low-effort SOTA upgrades. All prefixed dna_* / /api/v1/dna/* (additive-only).

    • Replace binary Gemini with VideoReward + VisionReward · 9-dim + 64-question judge · new table dna_video_quality_scores_v2
    • mSPRT always-valid inference on /api/v1/dna/experiments/* · anytime-stop with Λt > 1/α
    • CUPED variance reduction · pre-experiment covariate adjustment · −50% variance target
    • MLflow champion/challenger aliases · McNemar paired test + guardrail metrics · new service dna-model-registry
    • DVC + lakeFS branching on dna_corpus_v{N} · commit on every weekly extraction
    Exit: 9-dim reward live · mSPRT stops ≥ 3 experiments early · MLflow promotes one challenger · lakeFS branches per run
  11. W 35–40

    Phase 11 · Core SOTA infra (Sprint 2)

    SOTA

    VLM judge panel, causal attribution, off-policy evaluation, and hierarchical Bayes for portfolio intelligence.

    • VLM ensemble judge · Gemini 2.5 Pro + GPT-4o + Qwen2.5-VL-72B · Elo + Bradley-Terry consensus → dna_judge_panel table
    • DoubleML CATE attribution · replaces correlation · cross-fit nuisance models · unbiased creative-DNA effect on GMV
    • OBP β*-IPS off-policy evaluation · logged propensities → evaluate 100+ policy variants / week offline · endpoint /api/v1/dna/ope/*
    • ebmTS hierarchical bandits · brand → L3 → L2 → L1 partial pooling · 5–10× cold-start speedup · schema upgrade on dna_category_weights
    • RLAIF constitutional judge · content constitution (hook-3s, product-8s, social-proof, CTA, trust) · auto-generates preference pairs nightly
    Exit: judge panel agreement ≥ 0.80 · OPE evaluates ≥ 100 policies / wk · new brand cold-start < 20 imps to stat-sig · 500+ RLAIF pairs / day
  12. W 41–50

    Phase 12 · Advanced capabilities (Sprint 3)

    SOTA

    Combinatorial bandits, contextual policy, preference alignment loop, reward-hacking defense, and meta-learning.

    • CMAB-MT combinatorial bandits · attribute super-arms with semi-bandit feedback · captures hook × CTA × music interactions
    • NeuralUCB contextual bandits · 136-d context (brand × category × time × product × creator) on encoder hidden features
    • VideoDPO weekly fine-tune loop · winners→chosen, near-miss→rejected · TRL DPOTrainer with KL anchor to frozen ref
    • ORPO χ² occupancy regularization · proxy-true gap monitor · RM-ensemble disagreement alerts · anti-Goodhart defense
    • MAML / hierarchical-Bayes meta-learning · one cross-brand meta-model · O(10) fine-tune steps on new brand vs O(200)
    • MOL-TS Pareto bandits · joint GMV × safety × completion × new-customer · time-varying operator weights per campaign
    Exit: CMAB captures ≥ 1 attribute-interaction winner · VideoDPO weekly cadence stable · ORPO pins proxy-true gap ≤ 0.10 · meta-learned priors beat flat priors on 3 new-brand cold starts
  13. W 51+

    Phase 13 · Convergence endgame (Sprint 4)

    SOTA

    Indistinguishability (GAD) + 3-phase curriculum + UCB budget allocator with global ROAS floor.

    • GAD minimax training · discriminator co-evolves with generator · Nash equilibrium at chance-level accuracy · the principled indistinguishability target
    • 3-phase curriculum self-play · Phase 1 SFT on DPP corpus → Phase 2 RLAIF+VideoDPO → Phase 3 GAD · automatic phase transitions on metric triggers
    • UCB portfolio budget allocator · global ROAS-floor constraint · PID pacing trim · sorted-greedy allocation across 50+ brands
    • DPP diversity + hard-negative mining on training corpus · replaces popularity-biased top-N · dppy Gibbs sampling over InternVideo2 embeddings
    • PRIME implicit process rewards · per-step (per-clip) credit assignment · free extraction from ORM without step labels
    Exit: discriminator accuracy ≤ 0.55 sustained 2 rounds · portfolio ROAS ≥ 4.0× with all-brand floor · curriculum auto-advances through all 3 phases on production cadence
