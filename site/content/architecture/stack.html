Ownership split

DNA layer (Neon tables, /api/v1/dna/*) is owned by the ML track. ScriptSage monolith, queue-app, and engines stay under Platform Engineering — DNA reads/writes only through versioned interfaces, never patches in place.

Invariant

No production generation path is rewritten. vertex_standard, pollo_standard, seedance_standard remain the default; DNA configs are routed only by explicit A/B selection or tenant opt-in.

Compliance handoff

Generated videos land in GCS, then are scored against the DNA rubric AND voice-lock composite (≥0.88 production target). Either gate can reject. Rejections feed dna_video_analyses with failure reasons for root-cause analysis.
