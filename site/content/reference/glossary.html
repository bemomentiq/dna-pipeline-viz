16 · Glossary

Every term you'll see in the ledger

Short, operator-grade definitions for the acronyms and domain-specific words used across the pipeline.

DNA
The synthesized profile of what makes top-seller videos work in a category — frequencies, winning arms, and GPM correlations. Not a model. A versioned config object.
GPM
Gross merchandise per mille. Revenue (USD) divided by impressions × 1,000. Core reward signal.
Thompson Sampling
A Bayesian bandit algorithm that samples from each arm's posterior to choose the next draw. Naturally balances explore vs exploit.
Beta posterior
The distribution over success-rate for a single arm. Parameters α (successes) and β (failures). Mean = α/(α+β).
IDS
Indistinguishability Score. How close a generated video is to Kalodata top-sellers across 6 judge ensembles. 0.80+ is ship-ready.
JS divergence
Jensen–Shannon divergence. Symmetric distance between two probability distributions (e.g. last-week profile vs this-week). Drift alert at ≥ 0.10.
Composite rubric
Weighted average across 7 dimensions (hook, duration, caption, music, CTA, demo, B-roll). Ship gate at 0.70.
Voice composite
6-dim voice-lock score: F0 drift, RMS, first-chunk silence, langdetect, embed cosine, crossfade. Gate at 0.88 — strictest gate in the pipeline.
Additive-only
Core invariant: the DNA pipeline may add new tables, configs, endpoints, or columns, but may never modify an existing one. Enforced by CI.
Canary
Low-traffic slice (10%) of live jobs that receive a new policy. Side-by-side with a matched control. First real-world gate after shadow-only.
Shadow run
Generation with scoring but no distribution. Used to calibrate new policies against historic logs before risking real traffic.
Prior blending
When a category has n < 50, its bandit is initialized from a weighted mix of related categories' posteriors. Prevents cold-start thrash.
Drift auto-reset
Bandits reinitialize to profile frequencies after 3 consecutive same-direction JS-divergence weeks. Prevents stale posteriors.
Signal floor
1,000 views minimum before a video's GPM can update any bandit. Keeps low-impression noise out of the learning loop.
Category floor
20 videos minimum before a category has its own profile. Below this, it inherits blended priors.
Champion / Shadow
Champion is the live policy; shadow is the proposed replacement. Shadow runs off-policy until it beats champion statistically.
