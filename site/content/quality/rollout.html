1. 0%

    Shadow-only

    DNA configs generate variants but nothing ships. Scores are logged for calibration. Duration: 1–2 weeks.

    Promote when: compliance median ≥ 0.75 across 3 categories, voice composite ≥ 0.88 on 100% of chunks.
  2. 10%

    Canary

    10% of new jobs routed to DNA. Standards still receive 90%. Canary side-by-side with a matched control cohort.

    Promote when: 2-week canary shows DNA GMV ≥ standard GMV with p < 0.2, no rubric regression.
  3. 33%

    Split test

    One-third routed to DNA. A/B harness active. Per-category lift tracked separately.

    Promote when: DNA beats standard on GMV-per-product by ≥ +10% in majority of categories at p < 0.1.
  4. 66%

    Default + opt-out

    DNA becomes default; manual override preserves standards. 1-week bake.

    Promote when: zero client escalations, GMV lift sustained, drift alerts clear.
  5. 100%

    Steady-state

    All new jobs go through DNA. Standards kept as emergency rollback target for 90 days.

    Rollback trigger: GMV lift < 0% for 2 consecutive weeks, or P0 additive-guard violation.

One-command rollback

Every ramp step is reversible via feature flag. DNA rollout is fully additive, so rollback is a traffic-routing change — not a data migration.

# Immediately route 100% of new jobs back to standards
FF dna_pipeline_share = 0

# Freeze bandit updates (preserves posteriors for post-mortem)
FF dna_bandit_writes = false

# Drain in-flight DNA jobs (they complete normally; no new ones)
FF dna_accept_new = false
