15 · Unit economics & ROI

Cost per decision, payback by tier, marginal ROI by phase

Every autonomous decision has a unit cost. Variants cost ~$4 to generate, judge ensembles cost ~$0.08 per scoring, DPO rounds cost ~$180 in H100 hours, OPE estimations cost pennies. Plot those against expected GMV lift and every roadmap phase has a defensible payback window before it is enabled for each brand tier.
