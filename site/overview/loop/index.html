Closed-loop training, not a one-shot ETL

The pipeline is additive to the existing content platform — every DNA artifact (dna_*.json configs, dna_* tables, /api/v1/dna/* endpoints) is net-new. The loop starts at Kalodata, flows through Gemini vision analysis, lands in Neon as a versioned DNA profile, drives generation configs, gates outputs on compliance, ships to TikTok, and returns performance back to Thompson Sampling bandits that update every attribute arm.
