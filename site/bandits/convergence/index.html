Why this loop compounds

  1. Every stage produces a durable artifact — corpus version, judge annotations, bandit posteriors, MLflow model, DPO fine-tune, GAD discriminator. Nothing is thrown away.
  2. Three independent anti-overfit safeguards: ORPO occupancy regularization + RM-ensemble disagreement monitor + GAD co-evolving discriminator. Reward hacking requires defeating all three.
  3. OPE decouples policy eval from traffic — 100+ policy variants evaluated per week without spending a single impression.
  4. Hierarchical Bayes transfers every brand’s learnings to every other brand via shared L1/L2/L3 priors. Each new brand launches with portfolio-level intelligence baked in.
  5. The generator itself improves weekly via VideoDPO — the winning distribution becomes the next-round prior. Templates converge toward high-GMV content without human curation.
